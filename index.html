The consultant

Tamara Medina Sapovalova founded Sapovalova Solutions after two decades spent advising businesses across Latin America and Europe on market entry, partnerships, and operations. That work taught her something simple: most organizations don't have a strategy problem — they have an execution problem, and most of that execution is still done by hand.

Sapovalova Solutions brings that same care to AI automation. Every engagement is led personally by Tamara — no account handoffs, no junior bench quietly doing the work. You have direct access to the person who designed your system, for as long as you need her.

The approach is honest, always: if automation isn't the right move for a given problem, that's exactly what you'll hear. The goal is fewer, better systems that make your life easier — not more software to manage.
